Robots are gonna take over!

Hi, I'm Kapilesh. I work in the Android team at intive. Originally from New Delhi, I did my graduation from Delhi Technological University under the B.Tech. programme in Mechanical and Automotive Engineering. Rather disillusioned by traditional MechE courses, I decided to undertake a number of extra-curricular projects encompassing competitive coding, artificial intelligence, biodiesel extraction, hydraulic suspensions, general relativity, cosmology, quantum physics, philosophy, hoping to find what I like. I was eventually drawn to software engineering due to its versatility and its ability to solve problems in an automated fashion reliably and make life easy.

In the past few decades, softwares essentially has solved countless real world problems and has grown exponentially to pervade almost every industry. Mobile technology has taken a huge leap and has become an inseparable part of life for most of us.

Meanwhile, extensive research and technological advancements in the field of Machine Learning has further advanced our Limited Memory AI capabilities

I hope to be on the right side of this revolution and contribute as much as I can to our technological evolution. It rhymed! :D.

In my free time I like to explore new music, figure out some new tunes on my guitar, go hiking, stargazing and plenty of other things depending on who's around and what's happening. Recently, I also got into learning Chromatic Harmonica.
